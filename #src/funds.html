Secyrity funds

All monetary funds, with which our customers top up the deposit, will only be used for conducting trade transactions. All other expenses necessary for the provision of brokerage services, development of innovative products, provision of high-quality service, hedging transactions, improvement of software and security tools, the company bears at the expense of its own current capital.

The company management exercises continuous control over the intended use of funds. For this purpose, we have decided to create a risk management committee, whose duties would be to ensure the safety of the customer's and investor's funds, to review applications and make decisions on increasing the limits on transactions, to monitor the adherence to official rules of the company.

In the event of the discovery of facts testifying about unauthorized access of unauthorized persons to means of finance, the customer or the investor is obliged to change the password of his/her personal account and the used e-mail, to inform the customer support so that its members could take all necessary measures.

Security assurance

Before making the deposit, we recommend investors to carefully examine documents regulating the solution of all financial issues arising during the use of this platform. May we remind you that only full compliance with all the rules will allow us to ensure the safety of your funds. The company, in turn, undertakes to satisfy all obligations to the customer and the investor in a timely manner and in full.

Ways to protect financial transactions

All data concerning transactions conducted by the customer of the company is transmitted through an encrypted channel and using the SSL Protocol. All received information processing is carried out according to modern security standards, including PCI DSS. Customers and investors are advised to occasionally check the security of the connection (green icon with a lock in your browser).

To ensure maximum protection of deposit and withdrawal transactions, we cooperate exclusively with reliable electronic payment systems, such as Qiwi, WebMoney, Yandex.Money, BITCOIN, PerfectMoney, Payeer, AdvCash, Epayments, and Coinpayments, as well as with other aggregators of partners. Furthermore, our customers and partners can deposit and withdraw funds using bank cards or bank accounts.
